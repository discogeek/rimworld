# Galactic Rim Roadmap

Unofficial fan expansion plan. Phases can ship independently.

## v0.1 (done)
- Blasters, armor, two factions, Force Push, pack beast, utility droid POC

## v0.2 — Art & polish (next)
- Transparent high-res item icons (128×128 pixel art, no baked backgrounds)
- Custom utility droid body sprites (astromech-inspired silhouette)
- Worn apparel graphics (directional pawn overlays)
- Better blaster/projectile VFX

## v0.3 — Force & classes (SWTOR-inspired)
- **Light / Dark alignment** tracked per colonist (not just a trait)
- **Advanced classes** (pick one path after Force-sensitive):
  - Guardian / Sentinel / Sage (Light)
  - Juggernaut / Marauder / Sorcerer (Dark)
- Class kits: 2–3 abilities each + passives
  - Light: Force Push, Force Barrier, Battle Meditation
  - Dark: Force Choke, Force Lightning, Drain Life
- Ability unlock via use, meditation, or simple level track

## v0.4 — Ideology integration
- Memes: **Order of the Force**, **Sith Doctrine**, **Mandalorian Creed**
- Precepts for light/dark conduct, droid rights, blaster sanctity
- Role requirements: Force Knight, Shadow Acolyte, Quartermaster
- Rituals: meditation, initiation, hunt the fallen

## v0.5 — Terrain & world
- Tatooine-style arid tileset / sandy soil variant
- Ice / tundra “Hoth” weather tint hooks
- Mustafar volcanic floor (Anomaly/Odyssey terrain hooks if feasible)
- Biome patches for faction outposts

## v0.6 — Races & xenotypes
- Twi’lek / Zabrak / Chiss-style **xenotypes** (head horns, skin color channels)
- Optional **cybernetic** augment defs
- Faction-specific pawn kind weights

## v0.7 — Droids & mechs
- Protocol, astromech, battle droid lines (Biotech mechanoids)
- Craftable at fabrication bench / mech gestator recipes
- Droid personalities via Ideology or trait chips

## Design notes
- Keep **Harmony + optional DLC** pattern (`MayRequire` for Biotech/Ideology/Royalty)
- **Compatibility first**: additive defs/patches, `loadAfter` VE + Dubs, no faction rethemes
- Avoid Lucasfilm trademarks in package metadata; use “inspired” naming
- Replace vanilla mechanoid placeholder graphics before Workshop release

## v0.2 — Compatibility & polish (in progress)
- Transparent item icons, real Biotech utility droid
- COMPATIBILITY.md + safe biome patches
- Optional: mod settings to disable Galactic factions on new worlds
